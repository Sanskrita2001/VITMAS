ABOUT VIT

VIT, Vellore is among the most revered and reputable education institution which holds the rare distinction of being one of the few colleges to impart world-class education and in parallel, encouraging co-curricular activities which help in the overall development and transformation of students into responsible adults with a strong ethical core and rational outlook.

Vellore Institute of Technology was the birth-child of the relentless perseverance of our respected Chancellor, Dr. G. Viswanathan, whose objective since the very inception of this institution has been to provide an environment that would help foster intellectual aptitude, enhance human perception and create a utopic society of extra-ordinary professionals capable of conjuring innovative, out-of-the-box ideas and also possessing the skills required to implement them, in order to help society by and large.

ABOUT VITMAS

VITMAS (VIT Mathematical Association) is one of the most distinguished & diligent technical chapter in VIT. We are not just an organization, we are an enthusiastic family, united by one cause, working towards making technology more accessible, comprehensible & implementable.

The students of VITMAS have been working on projects related to web development, graphic designing, machine learning, IOT, app development and a lot more, and has been organising various outreach programs to educate the underprivileged as much as our humble span of knowledge permits. Apart from this, VITMAS also boasts of excellent connections in real time industries and also many faculty coordinators – one of the few chapters in VIT to possess so.

The students of VITMAS happen to be great learners and skilled technicians – who’ve conquered multiple hacks in and around VIT, won commendable research awards and also have a lot of experience and projects at hand to share to budding engineers.

Our Ideologies

  • Impart Knowledge
    In VITMAS, the primary goal is to impart knowledge and buttress innovation in all the fields we deal with as aforementioned, and we aim to achieve the same by providing numerous platforms and opportunities for students from all fields and walks of life to learn, build and enhance their skills.
  • All Round Developemnt
    We believe in practical learning and strive for all round development of a student in terms of everlasting learning, career development technical skills and professional networking & conduct.
