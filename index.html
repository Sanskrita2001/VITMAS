ABOUT VIT

VIT, Vellore is among the most revered and reputable education institution which holds the rare distinction of being one of the few colleges to impart world-class education and in parallel, encouraging co-curricular activities which help in the overall development and transformation of students into responsible adults with a strong ethical core and rational outlook.

Vellore Institute of Technology was the birth-child of the relentless perseverance of our respected Chancellor, Dr. G. Viswanathan, whose objective since the very inception of this institution has been to provide an environment that would help foster intellectual aptitude, enhance human perception and create a utopic society of extra-ordinary professionals capable of conjuring innovative, out-of-the-box ideas and also possessing the skills required to implement them, in order to help society by and large.

ABOUT VITMAS

VITMAS (VIT Mathematical Association) is one of the most distinguished & diligent technical chapter in VIT. We are not just an organization, we are an enthusiastic family, united by one cause, working towards making technology more accessible, comprehensible & implementable.

The students of VITMAS have been working on projects related to web development, graphic designing, machine learning, IOT, app development and a lot more, and has been organising various outreach programs to educate the underprivileged as much as our humble span of knowledge permits. Apart from this, VITMAS also boasts of excellent connections in real time industries and also many faculty coordinators – one of the few chapters in VIT to possess so.

The students of VITMAS happen to be great learners and skilled technicians – who’ve conquered multiple hacks in and around VIT, won commendable research awards and also have a lot of experience and projects at hand to share to budding engineers.

COVID-19 Contact Tracer

In public health, contact tracing is the process of identification of persons who may have come into contact with an infected person ("contacts") and subsequent collection of further information about these contacts. By tracing the contacts of infected individuals, testing them for infection, treating the infected and tracing their contacts in turn, public health aims to reduce infections in the population. Diseases for which contact tracing is commonly performed include tuberculosis, vaccine-preventable infections like measles, sexually transmitted infections (including HIV), blood-borne infections, some serious bacterial infections, and novel infections (e.g. SARS-CoV and SARS-CoV-2). The current outbreak of the Novel Crorona virus(COVID-19) also comes under the category of novel infections and thus is one of the infections where contact tracing can prove extremely useful.

The aim of this project is to build an application which can be used by the Health Ministry Of India to trace potential carriers of the virus and contain the outbreak.Although this application has some vulnerabilties and limitations , it can prove extremely useful to trace the source of the infection, locate places where the infected person has been to and trace other potentially infected people.

The sole purpose of the application is prevent the COVID-19 from spreading and infecting people across the nation. The application is not meant to be used to collect user data for commercial benefit or for private use.The application is made to save lives and swiftly contain the outbreak so that the situation can be resolved in minimum time.

Instagram Bot

This is an attempt to make a bot that could behave and act as a normal user . The bot can like posts , comment on them and is also capable of following people. The Bot is composed of four different sub-bots , each of which has a seperate function to perform. It is also necessary to mention that instagram monitors the activity of accounts and blocks their action if they appear to be suspicious. That is why there is a limit to the number of actions performed by the bot in one session.

    The sub-bots and their functions are as follows:
  1. Login Bot:This bot is responsible for logging in to the instagram account.
  2. Exploration Bot:This bot is responsible for getting information about the top accounts and hashtags on Instagram which could be used later.
  3. Commenting Bot:This bot uses the information gathered by the exploration bot and searches for the most popular accounts and hashtags . After searching for the accounts , it starts liking and commenting on the posts one by one.The comments are also scrapped from the internet and randomly used.
  4. Follow Bot:This bot is responsible for following accounts as suggested by the platform.

Self Gratitude Journal

It’s no secret that showing gratitude is good for you. As well as being backed by science, leaders such as the Dalai Lama are also big fans, so it’s no wonder that more of us are taking the time to be thankful for what we have in our lives. One of the best ways to show thankfulness on a daily basis is to keep a gratitude journal.

While regular journaling has been proven to improve your well-being, making a point of jotting down all the things that you are grateful for can take the experience to a whole new level. Keeping a gratitude journal is easy and on the simplest level it involves making a note of the things that you are grateful for that day. You can decide how long your list is each day and how much detail you want to go into. The great thing is that it’s an easy habit to form and after a while, you are left with a collection of inspiring material to look back on when you are in need of a pick-me-up.

“Gratitude makes sense of our past, brings peace for today, and creates a vision for tomorrow.” – Melody Beattie

Classification of celestial objects

The Sloan Digital Sky Survey has created the most detailed three-dimensional maps of the Universe ever made, with deep multi-color images of one-third of the sky, and spectra for more than three million astronomical objects. It allows to learn and explore all phases and surveys - past, present, and future - of the SDSS.

The dataset consists of 10,000 observations of space taken by the SDSS. Every observation is described by 17 feature columns and 1 class column which identifies it to be either a star, galaxy or quasar.The dataset can be found here.
